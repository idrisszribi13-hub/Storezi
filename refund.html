↩️ Refund Policy

At ZI Store, we strive to ensure our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases. This policy outlines our procedures for returns and refunds.

1. Return Period

You may return items within 30 days from the date of delivery. To be eligible for a return, the product must be in its original, unused condition and in its original packaging.

2. How to Initiate a Return

To start the return process, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team via email at support@zistore.com or via our "Contact Us" page.
  2. Provide your order number and a brief explanation of the reason for the return.
  3. Once approved, we will provide you with clear return instructions.

3. Refund Terms

  • Full Refunds: Provided for defective, damaged, or incorrect items received.
  • Change of Mind: If you decide to return an item due to personal preference, a 20% restocking fee may apply.
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error on our part.
  • Digital Products: Digital goods, such as software licenses or keys, are non-refundable once activated or downloaded.

4. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund within 2-5 business days. You will receive an email notification once the transaction has been completed.

5. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are excluded from our refund policy:

  • Products that have been used, altered, or damaged after delivery.
  • Digital products that have already been downloaded or activated.
  • Unauthorized transactions (please contact your financial institution for such cases).

If you have any further questions, please feel free to reach out to us at support@zistore.com.
