🔒 Privacy Policy

At ZI Store, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide: Such as your name, email address, and phone number when you register or contact us.
  • Usage data: Information about your interaction with our site, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and duration of your visit.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • Personalize your user experience and the advertisements you see.
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns to optimize site performance.
  • Communicate with you regarding your orders, inquiries, or support requests.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information with trusted third parties to assist in our operations, including:

  • Google: We use Google Analytics for traffic analysis and Google AdSense to serve ads. Google uses cookies to provide personalized advertising based on your interests.
  • Service Providers: Including hosting partners, payment processors, and analytics tools.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly.
  • Performance & Analytics: To help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to display relevant ads tailored to your preferences.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. To learn more about how Google manages data for advertising, please visit the Google Advertising Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request the correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Request the transfer of your data (data portability).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: support@zistore.com.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
